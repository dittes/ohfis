About Case Conversion

Have you ever typed a document and realized that you accidentally left caps lock on? Or did you want to capitalize the first letter of every word in a title?

Instead of retyping everything, use this tool to instantly convert your text. It supports all common transformations:

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters to capitals.
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to small letters.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of major words (simplified).
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes only the first letter of each sentence.
