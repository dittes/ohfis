Understanding Your Mortgage

A mortgage is likely the biggest financial commitment you'll make. A small difference in interest rates or loan terms can mean thousands of dollars saved or lost over the life of the loan.

Use this calculator to test different scenarios:

  • Shorter Term: Higher monthly payments, but significantly less interest paid.
  • Larger Down Payment: Reduces the principal and lowers monthly costs.
  • Interest Rate: See how current market rates affect your affordability.
