How VAT/Sales Tax works

Value Added Tax (VAT) or Sales Tax is a consumption tax placed on a product whenever value is added at each stage of the supply chain, from production to the point of sale.

Add Tax: Gross = Net * (1 + Rate/100)

Remove Tax: Net = Gross / (1 + Rate/100)
