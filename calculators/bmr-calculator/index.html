What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories required to keep your body functioning at rest. Equation used: Mifflin-St Jeor.

Knowing your BMR is the starting point for calculating your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE), which helps you plan weight loss or muscle gain diets.
