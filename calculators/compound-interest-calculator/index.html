The Power of Compound Interest

Albert Einstein famously called compound interest the "eighth wonder of the world." It allows your money to grow exponentially because you earn interest not just on your initial principal, but also on the accumulated interest from previous periods.

Start early and contribute regularly to maximize the effect. Even small monthly additions can lead to massive differences over 20 or 30 years.
